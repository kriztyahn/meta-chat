Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This application integrates with Facebook Messenger to provide automated customer assistant services. We respect your privacy and process data according to platform rules.

1. Data Collection

We receive public sender IDs and textual user interactions via Meta Webhooks. This data is strictly utilized to process and return automated responses.

2. Data Deletion

To request data deletion, users can delete their conversation thread or contact the page administrator directly. All temporary conversation memories will be cleared instantly.
